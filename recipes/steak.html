Steak Frites

Description:

If you are looking for the perfect recipe for date night, then look no further. This
easy to make recipe will be perfect for those cozy stay at home nights.

Ingredients:

  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil, or as needed
  • 2 hanger steaks
  • 2 tablespoons minced shallot
  • ¼ cup dry white wine
  • ¼ cup beef broth
  • 2 anchovy fillets, chopped, or more to taste
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh chervil
  • 1 teaspoon minced fresh thyme
