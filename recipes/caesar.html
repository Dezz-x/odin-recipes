Chicken Caesar Salad

Description:

What can beat a refreshing salad on a hot day? Add some chicken and
you have yourself a filling meal to get you through those steamy summer days.

Ingredients:

  • 6 cups tightly packed chopped romaine lettuce
  • 1 pound cooked boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into strips
  • ½ cup finely shredded Parmesan cheese, such as Cracker Barrel
  • ½ cup seasoned croutons
  • ¼ cup creamy Caesar dressing, such as Kraft
